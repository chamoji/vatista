> 2C > 5C > jc[9] > j.A > j.B > [A] Drill > [3][C] > crouch Fragmentum A > [3]
> A > A Laser (detonate) > crouch Fragmentum C > [2]B > EX Somersault

> 2]C[ > delay j.B (detonate)

> 5]A[ > 5A > 5B > 2]C[ > VO (detonate) > j.[9]C > j.FF > j.]8[2C > IW
